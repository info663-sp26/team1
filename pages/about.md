## About Venice Reborn

Venice Reborn: Cataloging the Masters is captured metadata constructing the relationships between works, representations of the works (photographs), and instantiations of those representations contained within a collection of digital image files of photographs of historical artworks from the I Tatti Archive, cataloged originally by Bernard Berenson; along with capturing rich metadata about those artworks themselves.  

MAP creators and information:
Adonis Fuentes: afuente6@pratt.edu
Matthew Henzy: mhenz221@pratt.edu
Delia Light: dlight5@pratt.edu
Daria Shulga: dshulga@pratt.edu
